# Airbnb Market Analysis: Chicago vs New Orleans

## Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to perform a comparative analysis of Airbnb listings between **Chicago** and **New Orleans**. Using Power BI's interactive visualization capabilities, the dashboard highlights pricing trends, room type distribution, neighbourhood concentration, availability, ratings, and listing characteristics to help understand the Airbnb market in both cities.

---

# Overall Insights

The dashboard reveals significant differences in Airbnb markets between Chicago and New Orleans. Chicago has a much larger and more expensive Airbnb market, while New Orleans offers comparatively affordable accommodations with fewer listings.

---

# KPI Analysis

### Total Listings

* Total Airbnb listings across the selected cities are **approximately 14,000**.
* Chicago contributes a larger share of listings compared to New Orleans, indicating a stronger Airbnb presence.

### Average Price

* Overall average listing price is **$471.98**.
* Chicago has a significantly higher average listing price (**$603.82**) compared to New Orleans (**$301.01**).
* This suggests Chicago targets both business and premium leisure travelers.

### Average Rating

* Average guest rating is **4.81/5**.
* Both cities maintain excellent customer satisfaction, indicating consistently good hosting quality.

### Average Availability

* Listings are available for approximately **245 days** per year on average.
* This indicates many properties are available throughout the year rather than only seasonally.

### Average Minimum Nights

* Average minimum stay requirement is **12.89 nights**.
* This suggests many hosts encourage longer bookings instead of short weekend stays.

### Average Reviews

* Listings receive an average of **66.61 reviews**, reflecting healthy customer engagement and booking frequency.

---

# Room Type Distribution

The donut chart shows Airbnb accommodation preferences.

### Observations

* **Entire Home/Apartment:** **81.05%**
* **Private Room:** **17.21%**
* **Hotel Room:** **1.46%**
* **Shared Room:** Less than 1%

### Interpretation

The Airbnb market is overwhelmingly dominated by entire homes or apartments, indicating that most guests prefer privacy and complete accommodation rather than shared spaces.

Private rooms still maintain a noticeable market share, mainly appealing to budget-conscious travelers.

Hotel and shared room options represent only a very small portion of listings.

---

# Listings by Price Range

The price bucket comparison reveals clear differences in pricing strategies.

### Chicago

* Strong concentration in the **$100–$199** price range.
* Large number of premium listings above **$500**.
* Higher representation in luxury accommodations.

### New Orleans

* More listings fall within the **$50–$299** range.
* Fewer luxury-priced listings.
* More affordable overall market.

### Interpretation

Chicago offers greater diversity across price segments, including luxury properties.

New Orleans focuses more on affordable and mid-range accommodations suitable for tourists seeking lower-cost stays.

---

# Conclusion

The comparative analysis demonstrates that Chicago and New Orleans exhibit distinct Airbnb market characteristics. Chicago has a larger, more premium-focused market with higher average prices and a concentration of listings in major commercial and tourist areas. In contrast, New Orleans offers a more affordable Airbnb market with listings distributed across multiple neighborhoods.

Power BI effectively transforms raw Airbnb data into meaningful visual insights through KPIs, charts, maps, and interactive filters. These insights can assist hosts in optimizing pricing strategies, help travelers make informed accommodation decisions, and support businesses in understanding regional Airbnb market dynamics.
